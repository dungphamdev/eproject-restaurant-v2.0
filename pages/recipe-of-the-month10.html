RECIPE OF THE MONTH

MASHED POTATOES WITH KASHKAVAL AND ROAST ONIONS

Difficulty Level
Veggie
Glutenfree

ingredients for 4 portions

1 large sweet potato
4 tbsp olive oil
4 handfuls kale
1 shallot
5 tbsp GAZİ natural yoghurt
1 tsp tahini
1 tbsp white vinegar
salt
pepper
juice and zest of 1 lemon
1 pomegranate
hazelnuts for topping

To serve

 cress, red pepper berries

Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C fan.

2. Dice the sweet potato and place on a baking tray with a little olive oil. Bake for approx. 25 minutes until al dente.

3. Remove the stems of the kale and chop the leaves into broad strips.

4. Heat a little olive oil in a frying pan, dice the shallots and sauté until translucent. Add the kale to the pan and sauté until it is at the desired consistency.

4. Slice the onion into rings and toss in the flour.

5. Heat oil in a pan and add the onion rings. Sprinkle a tablespoon of sugar over the top and allow the onion rings to roast for around 12 minutes.

6. Serve the mashed potato with grated kashkaval, the roast onions, fresh cress and a few red pepper berries.
